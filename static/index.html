The Challenge

The Growing Digital Fraud Crisis

India reported over 3 million cybercrime cases in 2025 alone. As digital payments and internet access expand rapidly, fraudsters are exploiting linguistic diversity and psychological vulnerabilities at an unprecedented scale.

📈
Exponential growth — Digital fraud cases in India grew 45% year-over-year, with UPI and OTP scams leading the surge.
🌐
Multilingual blind spots — Existing tools fail to detect scams in regional languages.
🧠
Psychological manipulation — Scammers leverage urgency, fear, and authority triggers that rule-based systems cannot identify.
