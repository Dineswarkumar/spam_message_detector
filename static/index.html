The Challenge

The Growing Digital Fraud Crisis

India reported over 3 million cybercrime cases in 2025 alone. As digital payments and internet access expand rapidly, fraudsters are exploiting linguistic diversity and psychological vulnerabilities at an unprecedented scale.

📈
Exponential growth — Digital fraud cases in India grew 45% year-over-year, with UPI and OTP scams leading the surge.
🌐
Multilingual blind spots — Existing tools fail to detect scams in multiple languages.
🧠
Psychological manipulation — Scammers leverage urgency, fear, and authority triggers that rule-based systems cannot identify.
Our Approach

Our Hybrid Protection Framework

RakshaSutra combines machine learning precision with rule-based explainability to build a layered defense that adapts to evolving fraud patterns.

01

ML Detection

Fine-tuned transformer models trained on Indian multilingual fraud datasets classify messages with contextual accuracy.

02

Psychological Pattern Analysis

Identifies manipulation triggers — urgency, fear, impersonation, reward baiting — using NLP behavioral markers.

03

Risk Scoring Engine

Generates an explainable composite risk score combining ML confidence, rule matches, and psycholinguistic signals.

04

Fraud Visualization

Interactive geospatial and categorical dashboards reveal fraud density, trend spikes, and regional hotspots across India.
